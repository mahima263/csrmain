Driving Impact through CSR Collaboration

At IIT Kanpur, we believe higher education institutes carry a responsibility beyond academics and innovation. Guided by our ethos, CSR here is not just a statutory requirement, but a shared commitment, where academia and industry collaborate to address society’s pressing challenges.

We partner with corporations to translate their CSR vision into high-impact, measurable outcomes blending innovation, empathy, and responsibility. Our curated programs align with national development priorities and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), leveraging faculty expertise, student ingenuity and expert outreach partners.

Join us as collaborators, mentors, and visionaries to create a future driven by academic excellence and inclusive development.

Why Partner with Us?

  • Frontier Research Leadership – Pioneering work in AI, Quantum Technologies, Cybersecurity, UAVs, MedTech, Sustainability, and Semiconductors.

  • Proven Impact – Successful deployments including AI-based PM grievance portal, indigenous UAVs, and affordable MedTech innovations.

  • Strong Innovation Ecosystem – SIIC and sectoral hubs nurturing 210+ startups and IP-driven ventures.

  • Trusted Collaborator – Partnerships with government, global industry leaders, and major healthcare institutions.

  • Societal Relevance – Solutions for Education, Sustainability, Healthcare, Clean Energy, Water, Transport Safety, and more—directly benefitting communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit on tax exemption for CSR contributions made to IIT Kanpur?

The institute is eligible to receive contributions under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, which offers 100% tax exemption on eligible donations.

Can companies receive reports or updates on the impact of their CSR contributions?

Yes, IIT Kanpur provides periodic impact reports detailing how CSR contributions are utilized, including progress updates, beneficiary data, and measurable outcomes of the supported projects. Transparency and accountability are key principles of our CSR collaborations.

Can corporates collaborate with IIT Kanpur beyond direct funding?

Absolutely. IIT Kanpur encourages strategic partnerships that go beyond monetary support including co-development of technologies, setting up student fellowships, infrastructure development, and innovation-driven CSR programs aligned with the company's thematic interests.

Will IIT Kanpur provide official documentation for CSR compliance and accounting?

Yes, IIT Kanpur issues official receipts and utilization certificates for all CSR contributions, along with detailed project reports as per CSR compliance requirements. These documents can be used for statutory filings and internal CSR reporting.

What are the thematic areas where IIT Kanpur accepts CSR support?

CSR contributions can support a wide range of areas including education, healthcare, sustainability, clean energy, skill development, rural upliftment, and innovation for social good. IIT Kanpur aligns CSR projects with the company's focus areas to ensure mutual value creation.

Does IIT Kanpur assist in developing customized CSR proposals?

Yes, the CSR and Development Office at IIT Kanpur collaborates closely with corporate partners to design proposals aligned with both the company's CSR policy and the institute's research expertise. This ensures high social impact.

Are CSR contributions monitored and evaluated for impact?

Yes, all CSR-funded projects at IIT Kanpur undergo periodic monitoring and evaluation to track progress, outcomes, and long-term impact. The institute shares these reports with corporate partners to maintain transparency and accountability.

How can a company initiate a CSR partnership with IIT Kanpur?

To begin a CSR partnership or discuss potential project areas, please write to dora_desk@iitk.ac.in or vp_development@iitk.ac.in. The IIT Kanpur CSR team will assist you through each step from proposal development to project execution and reporting.
